Calling all creatives
Welcome to Bitcamp's third annual COLORWAR design competition! COLORWAR features fast-paced design challenges for our Bitcamp participants to get their creative juices flowing.
How it works
Register
Register for Bitcamp 2016 by April 3
Colorwar is an annual event, held at Bitcmap, the hackthon at the University of Maryland, which will be held on April 8-10, 2016. You will have 36 hours to learn something new, and make something awesome.
Tweet
Submit your design by 11pm on Sunday, April 3
Create a visual that expresses your interpretatoin of Bitcamp's "Explore New Grounds" theme. Then show us that image on Twitter with the hashtag #COLORWAR @bitcmp.
Compete
4-6pm on Saturday, April 9 at Bitcamp
We'll select five finalists for the main event at Bitcamp. Contestants will battle it out through five rounds of knockout design challenges. Judges and the crowd will determine one victor.
Prizes
Everyone who submits will receive COLORWARstickers. The Top 5 contestants will all receive limited edition COLORWAR shirts and compete for moleskin notebooks, Copic markers, Pencils by FiftyThree, a Wacom graphic tablet, and more!
